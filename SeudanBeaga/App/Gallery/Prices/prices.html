We offer 4 types of package for children aged 2 - 5 years.

What We Offer

We are open 50 weeks of the year from 8am until 6pm. We close on some of the public holidays and public holidays are charged at half the daily rate.

Should your nursery fees be outstanding the nursery is under no obligation to keep your child’s placement in the nursery. 4 weeks written notice is required if you intend to withdraw your child from the nursery. The nursery closes at 6pm, if your child is not collected by 6pm a £5 charge incurs for up to the first 15 minutes and thereafter £5 for every further 5 minutes.
