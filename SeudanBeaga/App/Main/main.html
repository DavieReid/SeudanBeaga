Seudan Beaga

Established in October 2009, Seudan Beaga (Little Gems) is a nurturing, warm and friendly Nursery with a difference.

Aims of Seudan Beaga Nursery

  • To provide a safe, warm, happy, loving environment
  • To treat all children as individuals
  • To promote the development of children’s intellectual, physical, social and emotional
  • To provide quality care, based on a policy of equality
  • To foster a trusting and communicative relationship between children, parents, and staff
  • To provide an exciting Gaelic learning journey for children

Objectives of Seudan Beaga Nursery

“Seudan Beaga will provide a high standard of professional child care”

The nursery is bright, warm and welcoming and new children settle quickly.

The nursery has a wide variety of resources and equipment to stimulate the children in all areas of their development.

Continuity of care is enhanced by the stability of a low turnover of staff and a warm, caring and relaxed atmosphere.

A ‘key worker’ system operates throughout the nursery. The role of the key worker is to provide a safe and secure environment, have an awareness of the children’s background and stage of development.

The fluent Gaelic speakers at Seudan Beaga offer Gaelic support to parents and staff as well as children by providing Gaelic classes.
